Background & Research Focus

I am a Postdoctoral Research Associate at Texas Tech University , passionate about advancing technology through data-driven research and innovation. I am hold PhD in Computer Science from Texas Tech University where my research integrates deep learning, natural language processing, Large Language Models, Time Series Analysis, and human-computer interaction to enhance cybersecurity measures.

With a Master of Science in Software Engineering from Texas Tech University, I have extensive experience in detecting anomalies in time series data using deep learning algorithms. My current research interests include multidisciplinary research on social engineering attacks involving human factors, utilizing cutting-edge tools such as Large Language Models and Natural Language Processing (NLP).

Research Interests

Cybersecurity: Developing solutions to safeguard systems against cyber threats and vulnerabilities.
Deep Learning: Enhancing predictive accuracy through advanced neural network architectures.
Natural Language Processing: Utilizing NLP techniques for language analysis and generation.
Time Series Analysis: Analyzing temporal data to identify trends and anomalies.
Human-Computer Interaction: Improving user experience through effective interface design.
Large Language Models: Exploring applications of LLMs in text generation and summarization.
